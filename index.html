Our Sports Games

Enppi Clubs offer a huge variety of sports through 20 different academies; all applying the latest sports curriculum and led by professional coaches. Our aim is to continue building a legacy and support our heroes to reach the highest levels locally, regionally and globally.

E-football games

Football club

Enppi club competes in the Egyptian Premier League and plays home matches at Petrosport stadium in New Cairo. The club is related to Enppi (Engineering For The Petroleum And Process Industries), which was established in 1978.

Hand ball

Enppi club competes in Egyptian handball League is the premier professional handball league in Egypt. It was founded in 1958, Egyptian handball League was managed by EHF..
